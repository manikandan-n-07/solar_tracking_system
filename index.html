SOLAR TRACKING SYSTEM


BY

MANIKANDAN N


A solar tracking system with two Light Dependent Resistors (LDRs) optimizes solar panel orientation for maximum sunlight exposure. The LDRs detect light intensity, triggering motors to adjust panel angles accordingly, ensuring perpendicular alignment with the sun's position throughout the day. By constantly monitoring light levels, the system dynamically adjusts panel positioning, enhancing energy production efficiency. This dual LDR setup provides redundancy and accuracy, enabling precise tracking even in varying weather conditions. Ultimately, the system maximizes solar energy harvesting, enhancing renewable energy utilization and sustainability.
